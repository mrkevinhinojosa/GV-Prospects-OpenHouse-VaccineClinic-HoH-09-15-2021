We’ve got less than a month until the Granville Home of Hope Grand Prize Drawing, and tickets are going fast! When you purchase a Granville Home of Hope ticket, you will be entered for a chance to win the Grand Prize, a brand new Granville Canvas 9 home at Copper River Ranch, valued at over $500,000. But more importantly, every penny of every ticket purchased directly benefits 11 local nonprofits who are working to provide food, shelter, health care, and education to those in need in our community.


Once again, we are offering a limited number of two-ticket bundles, which includes one free entry into a drawing to win a 2-year lease on a 2021 Lexus UX 200, generously donated by Fresno Lexus. There will only be 2,250 bundles available to purchase, and as of this week, there are fewer than 300 bundles remaining.


Only 8,000 Granville Home of Hope tickets will be sold, and we are expecting to sell out for the fifth year in a row. The Lexus Drawing will be held on September 29th, and the Grand Prize Drawing will be held on September 13th. Don’t miss your chance to win a new home and to make a positive change in our community!
